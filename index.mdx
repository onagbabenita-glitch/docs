## How VidSpark Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a Mode">
    Pick **Image Generator**, **Image to Video**, or **Prompt to Video** from the welcome screen to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe Your Vision">
    Enter a prompt and fine-tune your result by selecting a style, mood, or motion intensity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate & Download">
    VidSpark creates your content in seconds — download it or keep iterating until it's perfect.
  </Step>
</Steps>
