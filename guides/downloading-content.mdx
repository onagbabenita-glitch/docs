Every creative session in VidSpark ends with output you can keep, share, or build on. Once your image or video finishes generating, a set of action buttons appears below the result — giving you the ability to download your creation, generate a fresh variation, carry your image forward into the next mode, or return to the beginning to start something entirely new.

## Downloading an Image (Mode 1)

After Google Imagen generates your image in Mode 1 (Image Generator), saving it to your device is a single click.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View your generated image">
    Once generation completes, your image appears full-width on screen. Take a moment to evaluate whether it matches your vision before downloading.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Download Image">
    Select the **Download Image** button displayed below the image. The download begins immediately — no additional dialog or confirmation required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your saved file">
    The image saves to your device in standard resolution. Check your browser's default downloads folder to locate the file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading a Video (Modes 2 and 3)

After Veo generates your video in Mode 2 (Image to Video) or Mode 3 (Prompt to Video), the video plays automatically so you can preview it before saving.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Preview the generated video">
    Your video plays automatically in the built-in player as soon as generation completes. Watch it through to evaluate the motion, style, and overall result.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Download Video">
    Select the **Download Video** button below the player. The download starts immediately and saves the video file to your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your saved file">
    The video saves in standard format at 5–8 seconds in length. You'll find it in your browser's default downloads folder, ready to share or edit in any compatible video tool.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Regenerating a Video (Mode 3 Only)

The **Regenerate** button is available exclusively in Mode 3 (Prompt to Video). It lets you produce a new version of your video without changing any of your settings.

- Clicking **Regenerate** uses the exact same prompt, style, and mood selections to produce a brand-new variation — no re-entry required
- This is most useful when the first result is close but not quite right, or when you want to explore how much variety Veo produces with the same inputs
- Every regeneration is unique — VidSpark sends a fresh generation request each time, so no two results will be identical, even with identical settings

## Continuing to the Next Mode

When you generate an image in Mode 1, VidSpark gives you the option to carry that image directly into Mode 2 without starting over.

- The **Turn this into a Video →** button appears below the generated image alongside the Download Image button
- Clicking it seamlessly passes your image into **Mode 2 (Image to Video)**, where it becomes the input for Veo's animation engine
- From there, you can write a motion description and select an intensity level to bring your image to life — no re-uploading or manual file transfer needed

## Starting Over

The **Start Over** button is always available after a generation completes, in all three modes.

- Clicking **Start Over** returns you to the main mode selector — the VidSpark home screen where you can choose between Image Generator, Image to Video, and Prompt to Video
- Your current prompt, style settings, and generated output are cleared from the interface
- **Your previously downloaded files are not affected** — anything already saved to your device remains intact

<Note>
  Downloaded files are saved to your default downloads folder. VidSpark does not store your generated images or videos in the cloud.
</Note>
