VidSpark's **Image to Video** mode is Mode 2 of the creative pipeline — it takes a still image and breathes life into it. Powered by **Google Veo**, this mode analyzes your image and applies AI-generated motion based on the movement description you provide. Whether you want gentle drifting clouds, rustling leaves, or explosive kinetic energy, you control exactly how much motion is applied through three intensity levels.

---

## How to Use Image to Video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Mode 2 — Image to Video">
    From the VidSpark welcome screen, tap or click **Image to Video** to enter Mode 2. Alternatively, click **Turn this into a Video →** from the [Image Generator](/modes/image-generator) output screen to arrive here with your image already loaded.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide your image">
    **Upload an image** from your device using the upload control, or simply continue with the image already passed in from Mode 1 — it will appear ready to use with no extra steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the motion you want">
    In the motion description field, explain how elements in your image should move. For example: *"the clouds start moving, wind blows through the trees."* Be specific about which elements move and how.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a motion intensity">
    Select one of three intensity levels — **Subtle**, **Medium**, or **Dynamic** — to control how pronounced the animation appears in the final video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Generate">
    Hit the **Generate** button and wait while Veo animates your image. You'll see the loading indicator:
    > *VidSpark is generating... ⚡*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch and download your video">
    Your animated video will play in the **large video player** on screen. Use the **Download Video** button to save it to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Motion Intensity Levels

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Subtle" icon="feather">
    Gentle, barely-there movement. Ideal for portraits, still-life scenes, and nature imagery where a soft, dreamlike quality is desired without distracting from the subject.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Medium" icon="wind">
    Noticeable motion with balanced natural flow. Great for most scenes — landscapes, cityscapes, and character images that benefit from life-like movement without going over the top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dynamic" icon="bolt">
    Energetic, dramatic movement with high visual impact. Best suited for action scenes, abstract imagery, or any composition where bold, expressive motion is the goal.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Passing an Image from Mode 1

VidSpark is built for a seamless creative flow between modes. When you finish generating an image in [Image Generator](/modes/image-generator), the **Turn this into a Video →** button automatically carries your image into Mode 2 — no downloading and re-uploading needed.

Your image appears pre-loaded and ready in the Image to Video workspace, so you can jump straight to writing your motion description. This handoff keeps your creative momentum intact and your workflow fast.

---

<Tip>
  Describe motion in terms of **specific elements** visible in your image — mention what moves, how fast, and in what direction for best results. For example, rather than *"make it move,"* try *"the water ripples outward from the center, and the tall grass on the left sways slowly in the breeze."*
</Tip>

<Note>
  After your video is generated, click **Start Over** at any time to return to the mode selector and begin a new creation.
</Note>
