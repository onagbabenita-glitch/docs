VidSpark's **Image Generator** is Mode 1 of the creative pipeline — your starting point for turning words into visuals. Powered by **Google Imagen**, it interprets your text description and produces a high-quality AI image rendered in the art style of your choice. Whether you're crafting a cinematic scene, a watercolor landscape, or a dark fantasy portrait, Image Generator gives you full creative control from a single prompt.

---

## How to Use Image Generator

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Mode 1 — Image Generator">
    From the VidSpark welcome screen, tap or click **Image Generator** to enter Mode 1.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the image you want to create">
    Type a detailed description of your desired image in the prompt field. For example: *"A lone wolf standing on a misty mountain at dawn."* The more specific your prompt, the more precise your result.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an art style">
    Select one of the seven available art styles to define the visual tone of your image. Each style shapes color, texture, and composition in a distinct way.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Generate">
    Hit the **Generate** button and wait while VidSpark works its magic. You'll see the loading indicator:
    > *VidSpark is generating... ⚡*
  </Step>
  <Step title="View, download, or continue">
    Your generated image will appear **full-width** on screen. From here you can download it or pass it directly to Mode 2 to bring it to life.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Art Styles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Photorealistic" icon="camera" href="/guides/art-styles">
    True-to-life detail, like a high-resolution photograph. Ideal for portraits, architecture, and natural scenes that need to feel tangible and grounded.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cinematic" icon="film" href="/guides/art-styles">
    Dramatic lighting and film-like color grading. Perfect for storytelling visuals with a big-screen, widescreen quality.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Anime" icon="star" href="/guides/art-styles">
    Japanese animation style with bold lines and vivid colors. Great for character art, fantasy scenes, and stylized action imagery.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Oil Painting" icon="palette" href="/guides/art-styles">
    Rich texture and classical brushwork aesthetic. Evokes the look of traditional canvas art with deep, layered tones.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Watercolor" icon="droplet" href="/guides/art-styles">
    Soft, translucent washes with fluid organic edges. Best for dreamy, delicate scenes and illustrations with a handcrafted feel.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dark Fantasy" icon="moon" href="/guides/art-styles">
    Gothic, moody atmosphere with dark color palettes. Ideal for mythological creatures, shadow-laden environments, and dramatic fantasy scenes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Minimalist" icon="minus" href="/guides/art-styles">
    Clean, simple forms with reduced visual complexity. Produces striking images that communicate through shape, space, and restraint.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What Happens Next

Once your image is generated and displayed, you have two options:

- **Download Image** — Saves the full-resolution AI image directly to your device.
- **Turn this into a Video →** — Passes your generated image seamlessly into [Image to Video](/modes/image-to-video) (Mode 2), where you can animate it with AI-driven motion using Google Veo.

The handoff between modes is instant — no re-uploading required. Your image travels with you.

---

<Tip>
  **Writing effective image prompts:** Be specific about your subject, lighting, and perspective for the best results. Instead of *"a forest,"* try *"an ancient redwood forest at golden hour, low-angle perspective, rays of light filtering through the canopy."* Mention mood, time of day, weather, and any key visual details you want included.
</Tip>

<Note>
  After your image is generated, click **Start Over** at any time to return to the mode selector and begin a new creation.
</Note>
