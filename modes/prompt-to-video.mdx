VidSpark's **Prompt to Video** mode is Mode 3 of the creative pipeline — a direct path from imagination to moving image. Powered by **Google Veo**, this mode generates original 5–8 second AI videos entirely from your text description, with no source image required. Dial in your vision by combining a **video style** and a **video mood** to shape everything from the visual aesthetic to the emotional atmosphere of the final clip.

---

## How to Use Prompt to Video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Mode 3 — Prompt to Video">
    From the VidSpark welcome screen, tap or click **Prompt to Video** to enter Mode 3.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your video scene">
    Type a detailed description of the video you want to generate. Use the placeholder as inspiration:
    > *"A futuristic city at night with flying cars and neon lights, cinematic 4K style."*
    Include environment, action, atmosphere, and any specific visual details to guide Veo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a video style">
    Select one of the six available video styles to define the overall visual treatment of your video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a video mood">
    Select one of the five available moods to set the emotional tone and pacing of your video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Generate">
    Hit the **Generate** button and wait while Veo builds your video from scratch. You'll see the loading indicator:
    > *VidSpark is generating... ⚡*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch your video">
    Your 5–8 second generated video will play automatically in the **large video player** on screen. Review it and decide what to do next.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or Regenerate">
    Click **Download Video** to save the clip to your device, or hit **Regenerate** to produce a new version using the same prompt, style, and mood settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Video Styles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Cinematic" icon="film">
    Film-quality visuals with dramatic angles, lens flares, depth-of-field blur, and professional color grading. Best for storytelling and narrative scenes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cartoon" icon="pen-nib">
    Bright, illustrated animation style with bold outlines, flat or cel-shaded colors, and expressive motion. Great for playful or fantasy content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Realistic" icon="eye">
    Naturalistic footage that mimics real-world camera work — handheld texture, true-to-life lighting, and believable physics for grounded, documentary-style videos.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Abstract" icon="shapes">
    Non-literal, artistic visuals driven by shape, color, and fluid motion. Ideal for music visuals, mood pieces, and experimental creative content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sci-Fi" icon="satellite">
    Futuristic environments with high-tech aesthetics — glowing interfaces, spacecraft, advanced architecture, and otherworldly lighting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Nature" icon="leaf">
    Organic environments, sweeping landscapes, and wildlife in motion. Captures the textures, colors, and rhythms of the natural world.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Video Moods

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Epic" icon="trophy">
    Grand, sweeping, and emotionally powerful. Pairs with bold music-style energy — think large-scale vistas, heroic movement, and climactic pacing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Calm" icon="cloud">
    Serene, slow-paced, and peaceful in atmosphere. Gentle transitions, soft light, and unhurried motion for meditative or ambient content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dark" icon="moon">
    Brooding, tense, and laden with mysterious undertones. Deep shadows, muted palettes, and an unsettling weight that keeps the viewer on edge.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Joyful" icon="sun">
    Bright, upbeat, and full of energetic feeling. Warm colors, quick cuts, and lively motion that communicates happiness and celebration.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mysterious" icon="question">
    Enigmatic, suspenseful, and otherworldly. Ambiguous visuals, unusual angles, and a sense that something just beyond understanding is unfolding.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Regenerating a Video

Not quite what you envisioned? Hit **Regenerate** to create an entirely new video using the same prompt, style, and mood — Veo will interpret your inputs differently each time, producing a fresh result. You can regenerate as many times as you like before downloading.

Regenerating is also useful for exploring the range of outputs a single prompt can produce. Small prompt tweaks between regenerations let you iterate quickly toward your ideal video.

---

<Tip>
  **Combine contrasting style and mood for unexpected results.** Some of the most striking videos come from pairings that shouldn't work on paper — try **Cartoon + Dark** for a stylized gothic animation, or **Nature + Epic** for a sweeping wildlife sequence that feels like a blockbuster trailer.
</Tip>

<Note>
  After your video is generated, click **Start Over** at any time to return to the mode selector and begin a new creation.
</Note>
