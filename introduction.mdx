VidSpark is an AI-powered creative app that turns your ideas into striking images and videos in seconds. Whether you're a designer looking for rapid concept exploration, a content creator building social media assets, or simply someone with a vision and no technical background, VidSpark gives you a direct line from imagination to finished output. Just type a description, pick a style, and let Google's most advanced generative AI models do the rest.

## Three Creative Modes

VidSpark is organized around three distinct creative workflows. Pick the one that matches your goal and get creating immediately.

## Powered by Google AI

VidSpark is built on two of Google's flagship generative AI models, each optimized for a different medium.

**Google Imagen** powers the Image Generator mode. Imagen is trained on a vast range of visual styles and can produce photorealistic photographs, painterly illustrations, graphic designs, and everything in between — all from a plain-language text prompt.

**Google Veo** powers both video modes. Veo understands motion, cinematography, and temporal storytelling, making it possible to animate a still image with believable motion or generate a fully original video clip from a scene description. The result is smooth, high-fidelity footage that reflects your chosen style and mood.

## Styles, Moods & Intensity

VidSpark gives you fine-grained creative control through a set of curated options in each mode. Every choice shapes the look, feel, and energy of your output.

**Image Generator — Art Styles**

When generating an image, choose one of seven art styles to define the visual treatment:

- **Photorealistic** — lifelike detail and natural lighting that looks like a photograph
- **Cinematic** — widescreen drama with film-grade color grading and depth
- **Anime** — bold lines and vivid color inspired by Japanese animation
- **Oil Painting** — rich, textured brushwork with classical depth and warmth
- **Watercolor** — soft washes and delicate edges with an illustrative, handcrafted feel
- **Dark Fantasy** — moody, atmospheric imagery with gothic and surreal undertones
- **Minimalist** — clean compositions, reduced palettes, and deliberate negative space

**Image to Video — Motion Intensity**

After providing an image and a motion description, select one of three intensity levels to control how much movement is applied:

- **Subtle** — gentle, almost imperceptible motion that adds life without distraction
- **Medium** — natural, fluid movement that feels organic and purposeful
- **Dynamic** — bold, energetic animation with strong visual momentum

**Prompt to Video — Video Styles & Moods**

When generating a video from a text prompt, combine a video style with a mood to shape the final result:

Video styles: **Cinematic**, **Cartoon**, **Realistic**, **Abstract**, **Sci-Fi**, **Nature**

Moods: **Epic**, **Calm**, **Dark**, **Joyful**, **Mysterious**

## Action Buttons

Every output in VidSpark is paired with contextual action buttons so you can download, continue, or start fresh:

- **Download Image** — saves your generated image to your device (Image Generator)
- **Turn this into a Video →** — sends your generated image directly to Image to Video mode
- **Download Video** — saves your generated video to your device (Image to Video and Prompt to Video)
- **Regenerate** — generates a new video from the same prompt and settings (Prompt to Video)
- **Start Over** — returns you to the mode selector from any output screen
