VidSpark is an AI-powered creative app that turns your ideas into striking images and videos in seconds. Whether you're a designer looking for rapid concept exploration, a content creator building social media assets, or simply someone with a vision and no technical background, VidSpark gives you a direct line from imagination to finished output. Just type a description, pick a style, and let Google's most advanced generative AI models do the rest.

## Three Creative Modes

VidSpark is organized around three distinct creative workflows. Pick the one that matches your goal and get creating immediately.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Image Generator" icon="image" href="modes/image-generator">
    Describe any image and choose an art style. Google Imagen renders your vision as a high-quality still image in moments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Image to Video" icon="film" href="modes/image-to-video">
    Upload or pass in an existing image, add a motion description and intensity level, and Veo brings it to life as a short animated video.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prompt to Video" icon="clapperboard" href="modes/prompt-to-video">
    Describe a full scene — including style and mood — and Veo generates a cinematic 5–8 second video clip directly from your words.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Powered by Google AI

VidSpark is built on two of Google's flagship generative AI models, each optimized for a different medium:

**Google Imagen** powers the Image Generator mode. Imagen is trained on a vast range of visual styles and can produce photorealistic photographs, painterly illustrations, graphic designs, and everything in between — all from a plain-language text prompt.

**Google Veo** powers both video modes. Veo understands motion, cinematography, and temporal storytelling, making it possible to animate a still image with believable motion or generate a fully original video clip from a scene description. The result is smooth, high-fidelity footage that reflects your chosen style and mood.

## What You Can Create

VidSpark is flexible enough to support a wide range of creative projects, including:

- **Concept art** for games, films, books, or product design
- **Social media clips** and short-form video content for any platform
- **Cinematic scenes** with custom lighting, mood, and camera style
- **Animated photos** that bring portraits or landscapes into motion
- **Illustrated story beats** for pitches, storyboards, or mood boards
- **Abstract or surreal visuals** for artistic experimentation
- **Branded content** with consistent style across image and video assets
- **Educational visuals** that make complex ideas tangible

<Note>
  VidSpark is designed for creatives of all skill levels — no coding or design experience needed.
</Note>
