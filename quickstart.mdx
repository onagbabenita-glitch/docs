Getting from idea to finished output with VidSpark takes less than two minutes. There's nothing to install, no accounts to configure, and no prior experience required. Open the app, describe what you want, and VidSpark's AI does the rest — whether you're creating a single image or a fully animated video scene.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open VidSpark">
    Navigate to the VidSpark app. You'll immediately see the bold welcome screen displaying **VidSpark ⚡** against a dark background, along with three clearly labeled mode options ready for you to choose from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Mode">
    Select one of the three creative modes: **Image Generator**, **Image to Video**, or **Prompt to Video**. Each mode is purpose-built for a different creative goal — generating a still image, animating an existing photo, or producing an entirely original video from a text description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe Your Vision">
    Type a description of what you want to create into the prompt field. The more specific and detailed your prompt, the closer the output will match your intent. Include subjects, settings, lighting, mood, and any other details that matter to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Style">
    Select the options that shape your output:

    - **Image Generator**: pick one of seven art styles — Photorealistic, Cinematic, Anime, Oil Painting, Watercolor, Dark Fantasy, or Minimalist.
    - **Image to Video**: describe the motion you want, then choose a motion intensity — **Subtle**, **Medium**, or **Dynamic**.
    - **Prompt to Video**: choose a video style (Cinematic, Cartoon, Realistic, Abstract, Sci-Fi, or Nature) and a mood (Epic, Calm, Dark, Joyful, or Mysterious).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click the **Generate** button and wait while the loading indicator displays **VidSpark is generating... ⚡**. Image generation typically completes in seconds; video generation may take a little longer depending on clip length and complexity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or Continue">
    Once your output is ready, use the action buttons to decide what to do next:

    - **Download Image** — saves your generated image to your device.
    - **Download Video** — saves your generated video to your device.
    - **Turn this into a Video →** — sends your generated image straight to Image to Video mode to animate it.
    - **Regenerate** — creates a new video from the same prompt and settings without going back (Prompt to Video only).
    - **Start Over** — returns you to the mode selector so you can begin a new creation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Try Image Generator first — generate an image, then click **Turn this into a Video →** to seamlessly send it to Image to Video mode. It's the fastest way to experience the full VidSpark creative pipeline in a single session.
</Tip>
